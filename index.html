Who I am ?

Hello, my name is Anthony Hong and welcome to my website. I enjoy innovation and love to create new ideas into a reality. Whether it be designing buildings, company brand, websites, you name it!

I have completed a specialized course in web and UX design in 2020 but before I dived into this industry, I was a studying in the field of architecture at the University of Auckland. After my architectural studies, I wanted to explore the field of business and had the opportunity to help and witness a business grow within a year. Whether you own a small cofee shop, a clothe shop or run a tech company, everything came back to business and thats what sparked my curiosity. "What is a company or a business?" "How is it formed?" A series of questions came to mind and to understand how these industry have formed, I wanted to be exposed to this new territory and learn the process from the ground up.

During the start up, there were many challenges my friends had to face and the most important thing I took from this was your surroundings, the environment of people you choose to work with and having the right qualities, work ethics and common vision. Skills are important and the basics, but I believe working as a team is more crucial because thats what holds everything together.

You can see my latest works on my github repository, please copy and paste this URL link: https://github.com/AnthonyFullStack?tab=repositories
